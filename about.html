About Me

Hi, I'm Sasawat and I love Sudoku Game!


I'm a computer programming and analysis student at Seneca College in Ontario. Originally from Thailand, I am enthusiastic about embracing new challenges and learning through continuous growth.


I hold a degree in Mechanical Engineering from Thailand, which has equipped me with a strong foundation in analytical thinking and problem-solving. Currently, I am expanding my knowledge and honing my programming skills at Seneca College, where I'm specializing in computer programming and analysis.


My journey in the world of puzzles began with a fascination for Sudoku, a captivating number puzzle that has improved my logical thinking and problem-solving skills. As a mechanical engineer, I appreciated the precision and order that Sudoku demanded, but my love for logical puzzles led me to explore computer programming and analysis as a new career path.


Today, I find joy in coding and developing websites, exploring the intricacies of algorithms, and solving complex programming challenges. Sudoku has played a significant role in shaping my logical mindset, making me a more analytical and detail-oriented problem solver in the realm of computer programming.


Join me on this journey as I share my experiences, projects, and insights into the world of Sudoku and computer programming. Together, let's explore the fascinating interplay between logic, problem-solving, and creativity in both the mechanical and digital realms!
